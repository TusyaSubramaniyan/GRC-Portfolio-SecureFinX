## What is a Statement of Applicability?

The SoA is a mandatory ISO 27001 document that lists all 93 Annex A controls and states:
- Whether each control **applies** to the organisation
- Whether it is **included** in the ISMS
- The **justification** for inclusion or exclusion
- **Implementation notes** for each control
