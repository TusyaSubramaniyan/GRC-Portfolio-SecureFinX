## 🏢 Client Overview

SecureFinX is a simulated 60-person fintech startup based in 
Chennai, India. They operate a mobile payment application with 
200,000 active users, storing Aadhaar-linked KYC data and 
payment transaction history on AWS Mumbai.

They engaged me as their GRC Consultant to:
- Assess their current security posture
- Identify and score information security risks
- Build their ISO 27001-aligned ISMS from scratch
- Write core security policies
- Conduct an internal audit and produce a formal report

## 📊 Project Deliverables

### 1️⃣ Risk Register
- 20 identified risks across technical, human, process & physical domains
- CVSS-aligned Likelihood × Impact scoring
- Risk levels: 4 Critical · 8 High · 8 Medium
- Risk treatment decisions for every risk (Treat/Tolerate/Transfer/Terminate)
- Risk heat map visualisation

### 2️⃣ ISO 27001 Gap Analysis
- All 93 Annex A controls assessed
- Status: Implemented / Partial / Not Implemented / N/A
- Findings and recommended actions for each gap
- Live summary counter of compliance status

### 3️⃣ Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- Documents which controls apply, which are excluded, and why
- Justification for inclusion/exclusion of each control
- Implementation notes per control
- Required document for ISO 27001 certification

### 4️⃣ Security Policies (5 documents)
| Policy | Document ID | ISO 27001 Reference |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Acceptable Use Policy | SEC-POL-001 | A.5.10, A.6.2, A.8.1 |
| Access Control Policy | SEC-POL-002 | A.5.15–A.5.18, A.8.2–A.8.5 |
| Incident Response Policy | SEC-POL-003 | A.5.26, A.5.27, A.6.8 |
| Data Classification Policy | SEC-POL-004 | A.5.12, A.5.13, A.5.14 |
| BYOD Policy | SEC-POL-005 | A.5.10, A.6.7, A.8.1 |

### 5️⃣ Internal Audit Report
- Formal audit conducted against ISO 27001:2022 Clauses 4–10
- Non-conformities documented with severity ratings
- Corrective action recommendations
- Executive summary for management

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

- ISO 27001 doesn't tell you HOW to secure your org — it tells you WHAT to prove
- The gap between policy and practice is where every non-conformity lives
- Risk treatment is a business decision, not just a technical one
- Writing 5 policies from scratch teaches you more than reading 50 articles
- Governance failure causes more breaches than technical failure
